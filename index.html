Register your cargo!

  • In order to start cooperation, you need to register the cargo indicating information about the contents of the cargo, its value and quantity. A also by attaching photographs to the application.
  • If it is necessary to check the goods for compliance with the quantity, then when When registering the cargo on our website, please indicate this in the “Note” column. This service is paid and amounts to 3 yuan per item in the order. Any additional cargo checks are discussed individually, including including their cost.
  • Before sending the goods to our warehouse in China, be sure to take care of to make a marking with your order number on our website, for each external box, so that we can determine exactly Your cargo. If you do not have the opportunity to do this yourself, then ask your supplier about this.
  • After sending the cargo to our warehouse in China, you must indicate the track number for tracking parcels in China for each items in the order. You can enter the tracking number on the order page. Track number is another measure to identify your cargo on our warehouse.
  • If you have registered the cargo according to our instructions and done everything correctly, then upon receipt of your cargo at a warehouse in China, the order status will change to "In stock".
